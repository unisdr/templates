UNITED NATIONS OFFICE FOR DIASTER RISK REDUCTION - UNISDR25 PAGES, 2007FREE

Extract from the final report of the World Conference on Disaster Reduction (A/CONF.206/6): The WCDR was held from 18 to 22 January 2005 in Kobe, Hyogo, Japan, and adopted the present Framework for Action 2005-2015: Building the Resilience of Nations and Communities to Disasters. The Conference provided a unique opportunity to promote a strategic and systematic approach to reducing vulnerabilities and risks to hazards. It underscored the need for, and identified ways of, building the resilience of nations and communities to disasters.
